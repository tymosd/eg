ABOUT US

Eurogerm USA is a subsidiary of Eurogerm S.A. in France. Eurogerm is an international bakery and milling ingredient company based in Dijon, France, with subsidiaries in USA, Spain, Germany, Italy, Mexico, Brazil, Columbia, Peru, South Africa, Senegal, Morocco and Lebanon.

Eurogerm is the only ingredient company with comprehensive technical understanding of wheat in the field to bread on the table. Eurogerm’s product include bakery and milling ingredients.

Eurogerm USA offers a wide range of standard and custom solutions involving functional and sensory ingredients for bread, pastry and sweet goods. Eurogerm’s highly technical approach has allowed its products to perform well in all types of products and conditions. Due to company’s CSR principles Eurogerm continues to develop new ingredients to meet clean label, non-GMO, Organic, vegan demands from the consumers.
